Introduction

Optimization is a programmic process to minimize or maximize a deifned objective function by tuning the deisgn variables bounded by a set of constraints. In the field of energy engineering, many problems can be discribed as an optimization problem:

  • Design its generation portoforlio for a powerplant that maximizes its revenue in the energy market.
  • Design the mixture of distributed energy sources plus storage systems in a micro-grid to minimized the payback term, and perhapse meet the green house gas emmission quota.
  • Control the charging and discharging sequences of a battery system, to minimize the energy cost.
  • Design a tracker to maximize the energy output from solar panels
  • Design an electric vechile charging scheme that aviods surplus charges during peak time.

Yet, many problems are a little more complex to define:

  • Deisgn a battery management system that prolong its cycle life.
  • Pick the next site of an energy storage system deployment that brings the most benefit to the grid.
